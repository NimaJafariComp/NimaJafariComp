## Research & Engineering Interests

My work sits at the intersection of **machine learning research** and **practical full-stack engineering**.  
I'm especially interested in building systems that are grounded, reliable, and useful in real-world settings.

### Core Areas
- **Full-Stack Engineering** — frontend, backend, APIs, databases, and cross-layer system design
- **AI Applications** — RAG pipelines, LLM features, retrieval, ranking, and orchestration
- **Machine Learning** — reinforcement learning, symbolic analysis, computer vision, and applied modeling
- **Robust & Reliable ML** — reproducibility, evaluation, corruption testing, and grounded outputs
- **Decision Systems** — strategy discovery, abstraction, optimization, and intelligent workflows
- **Vision & Perception** — pose estimation, sports-motion analysis, CNNs, and visual pipelines

### Engineering Interests
- **Backend systems** — APIs, service integration, database-driven functionality, and system reliability
- **Web and mobile products** — React web apps and React Native mobile development
- **AI product engineering** — tool-using systems, RAG, agentic workflows, and user-facing intelligence
- **Research-to-product translation** — turning experimental ideas into production-ready software
